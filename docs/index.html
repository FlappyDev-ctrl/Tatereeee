Ultimate Avoid Toolkit (Legit)

Fine-tune how the client keeps you out of freeze while staying fully legitimate. Every control listed below is available in the settings menu.

Movement

Legit Freeze Escape

  • Automatic freeze avoidance: enable tc_avoid_freeze to gently push your tee away from nearby freeze tiles without teleporting.
  • Custom detection bubble: adjust the radius with tc_avoid_freeze_distance to match your map and playstyle.
  • Responsive steering: fine-tune how assertively the helper takes over with the tc_avoid_freeze_strength slider so it only nudges when you want it to.
  • Safety hop: toggle tc_avoid_freeze_auto_jump to automatically hop in place when freeze tiles creep underneath you.
  • Prediction tuning: tweak frozen prediction margins and remove excess antiping ticks for a smoother unfreeze recovery.
  • Optional fast inputs: cut up to 20ms of delay while keeping a legitimate input stream.
Awareness

Visual Safety Nets

  • Freeze HUD & ghosts: track frozen teammates, reveal predicted/unpredicted ghosts, and highlight risky positions.
  • Player indicators: radial markers guide you to allies even when they are off-screen or behind walls.
  • Center guides: draw precise center lines and tee outlines to plan your next move.
  • Status intelligence: configurable status bar surfaces local time, ping, ammo, and armor at a glance.
Automation

Smart Legit Helpers

  • Auto voting: automatically vote against map changes when you are still progressing a run.
  • Contextual replies: send polite whisper responses to muted players or when the game is minimized.
  • Execute on join: run custom commands before connecting or when loading into a server.
  • War list sync: color-code chats, scoreboards, and indicators for your war groups.
Customization

Make It Yours

  • Legit visuals: refine tee trails, outline palettes, rainbow effects, and custom fonts for a consistent look.
  • Profile presets: store entire skin/name/clan/flag setups and swap them instantly.
  • Tiny tees & fake flags: adjust tee scale per player and simulate CTF colors for better visibility.
  • Chat filters: mute regex-matched spam and keep focus on the run.
